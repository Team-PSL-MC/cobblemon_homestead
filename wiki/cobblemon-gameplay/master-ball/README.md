#### üí° Tips for the Craft

* Powering the Array: Because a 5x5 grid requires significant Stress Capacity ($$SU$$), ensure your Create power source (Steam Engine or multiple Windmills) is robust before starting the craft.
* Automation: Use an AE2 Pattern Provider or Sophisticated Pipes to feed the ingredients into the Mechanical Crafters automatically.
* The Antimatter Bottleneck: The Antimatter Pellet is the hardest item to get. Focus on your Mekanism fission reactor early!
