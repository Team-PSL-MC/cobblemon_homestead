# Dimensions & Pokémon Spawns

## 🌲 The Twilight Forest
**The Realm of Eternal Dusk.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Ghost, Dark, and Ancient Paradox forms ($Past$ Paradox).

**How to Enter:**
1. Dig a 2x2 hole in the ground and fill it with water.  
2. Surround the edge with flowers, mushrooms, or tall grass (all 8 blocks).  
3. Throw a Diamond into the water. Lightning will strike, turning the water into a portal.

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** The Twilight Forest follows a strict progression. You cannot enter certain biomes (like the Dark Forest) until you defeat the boss of the previous area (like the Naga or Lich).

---

## 🌠 Eternal Starlight
**The Shimmering Abyss.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Future Paradox forms ($Iron$ variants).

**How to Enter:**
1. Find a Portal Ruin in the Overworld (use an Explorer's Compass if you have one).  
2. Defeat the Gatekeeper NPC guarding the structure to receive the Orb of Prophecy.  
3. Right-click the Portal Frame with the Orb to ignite the gateway.

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** This dimension is very status-effect heavy. Craft Swamp Silver gear early to resist the local debuffs.

---

## ☁️ The Aether
**The Kingdom in the Clouds.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Flying types, Dragons, and Fairies.

**How to Enter:**
1. Build a 4x5 frame of Glowstone (similar to a Nether portal).  
2. Use a Water Bucket on the inside of the frame to activate it.

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** Gravity is your biggest enemy here. Always carry a parachute or have a Pokémon with Fly or Levitate to avoid falling into the Overworld below.

---

## 🐝 The Bumblezone
**Inside the Hive.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Bug types (specifically Bees) and Honey-themed variants.

**How to Enter:**
1. Find a Bee Nest or Beehive in the Overworld.  
2. Throw an Ender Pearl into the hive.

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** Do not harm the bees or take honey without "Bee Queen" approval, or every mob in the dimension will become aggressive.

---

## 🌑 Deeper and Darker
**The Otherside.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Rare spawns (Legendaries/Shinies) have a much higher encounter rate here.

**How to Enter:**
1. Locate an Ancient City in the Deep Dark.  
2. Clear the Sculk Veins from the large "Center Portal" structure.  
3. Ignite the reinforced deepslate frame (usually by killing a Warden nearby or using an Otherside Key).

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** Sound is dangerous! Use Wool to dampen your footsteps and avoid attracting high-level hostile mobs.

---

## 🌸 The Feywild
**The Mythical Wilds.**

**Pokémon Spawns:** Fairy and Grass types.

**How to Enter:**
1. Craft a Feywild Portal (check JEI for the recipe, typically involving gold and flowers).  
2. Step through the shimmering rift.

⚠️ **Pro-Tip:** Time moves differently here. It is an excellent place to find "Day-only" or "Night-only" spawns depending on the eternal season of the biome you are in.

---

## ⛏️ Resource Dimensions

**Note:** These areas do not have unique Pokémon spawns but feature significantly higher ore density.

**JAMD (Just Another Mining Dimension):**  
A flat, mob-free paradise for heavy machinery. Enter via the Mining Portal block.

**JAMD Nether Mining:**  
Enter via Mining Portal placed in Nether.

**JAMD End Mining:**  
Enter via Mining Portal Placed in End.
