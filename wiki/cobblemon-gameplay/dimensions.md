🌲 The Twilight Forest

The Realm of Eternal Dusk.

    Pokémon Spawns: Ghost, Dark, and Ancient Paradox forms (Past Paradox).

    How to Enter: 1. Dig a 2x2 hole and fill it with water.
    2. Surround the edge with flowers, mushrooms, or tall grass (8 blocks total).
    3. Throw a Diamond into the water.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: Follow the progression! You’ll take environmental damage in biomes like the Fire Swamp or Snowy Forest until you defeat the previous area's boss (Naga → Lich → Minoshroom).

🌠 Eternal Starlight

The Shimmering Abyss.

    Pokémon Spawns: Future Paradox forms (Iron variants).

    How to Enter: 1. Locate a Portal Ruin in the Overworld.
    2. Defeat the Gatekeeper NPC to obtain the Orb of Prophecy.
    3. Right-click the Portal Frame with the Orb.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: This dimension is heavy on status effects. Craft Swamp Silver gear early to negate local debuffs.

☁️ The Aether

The Kingdom in the Clouds.

    Pokémon Spawns: Flying, Dragon, Fairy, and "Bright" themed Pokémon.

    How to Enter: 1. Build a 4x5 frame of Glowstone (like a Nether portal).
    2. Use a Water Bucket on the inside of the frame to activate it.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: Gravity is your biggest enemy here. Always carry a parachute or a Pokémon with Fly/Levitate to avoid falling into the Overworld below.

🐝 The Bumblezone

Inside the Hive.

    Pokémon Spawns: Bug types (specifically Bees) and Honey-themed variants.

    How to Enter: 1. Find a Bee Nest or Beehive in the Overworld.
    2. Throw an Ender Pearl into the hive.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: Do not harm the bees or take honey without "Bee Queen" approval, or every mob in the dimension will become aggressive.

🌑 Deeper and Darker

The Otherside.

    Pokémon Spawns: Rare Spawns (Legendaries/Shinies) have a higher base encounter rate here.

    How to Enter: 1. Locate an Ancient City in the Deep Dark.
    2. Clear the Sculk Veins from the "Center Portal" structure.
    3. Kill a Warden nearby or use the Otherside Key (depending on your config) to ignite the reinforced deepslate frame.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: Sound is dangerous here. Use wool to dampen your footsteps to avoid attracting high-level hostile mobs.

🌸 The Feywild

The Mythical Wilds.

    Pokémon Spawns: Fairy and Grass types.

    How to Enter: 1. Craft a Feywild Portal (usually involves flowers and gold/gems—check JEI for the specific mod recipe).
    2. Step through the shimmering rift.

    ⚠️ Pro-Tip: Time moves differently here. It is an excellent place to find "Day-only" or "Night-only" spawns depending on which season the biome is stuck in.
