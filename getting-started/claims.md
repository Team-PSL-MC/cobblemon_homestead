We use FTB Chunks to ensure your homestead remains yours. Your claim grows as you spend more time on the server!

#### How to Claim Land

1. Open the Map: Press `M` to open your Fullscreen Map.
2. Claiming Mode: Click the Claimed Chunks icon (the map/grid icon) in the top left corner.
3. Select Chunks:
   * Left-Click a chunk to claim it.
   * Shift + Left-Click a chunk to Force Load it (this keeps your Cobbleworkers and machines running even when you aren't standing there!).
   * Right-Click to unclaim.

#### üìà Expanding Your Territory

Everyone starts with a base amount of chunks, but you can earn more in three ways:

1. Playtime: You automatically gain additional claim and force-load permits the longer you play on the server.
2. In-Game Quests: Completing specific milestones in the Quest Book will grant "Claim Expansion" rewards.
3. Event Crates: Rare rewards from server events and crates can occasionally grant massive boosts to your claim limits.
