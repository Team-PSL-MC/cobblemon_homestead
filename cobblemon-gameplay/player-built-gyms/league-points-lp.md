#### ðŸ›¡ï¸ Anti-Exploit Rules

To keep the economy fair, the following rules are hard-coded into the League System:

* Speed-Run Protection: Gyms cleared in under 60 seconds will not grant rewards.
* Effort Check: Completions faster than 2 minutes receive reduced rewards. Take your time and enjoy the battle!
* Cooldowns: You can re-run a Player Gym every 12 hours, but Official Tinker Gyms have a 1-week (168h) cooldown.
