## 📝 How to Join the Contributors List

If you’ve contributed to the server, you can be added here:

1. Make a **Pull Request** updating this page with your name and contribution type.  
2. Include a **link to your PR, issue, or guide** if possible.  
3. Mention **your preferred role** (spawn editor, wiki, quests, etc.)  

We’ll review and merge contributions so your work is recognized publicly!
